[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Shadows dancing on the hardwood floor
I never wanted anyone more
Pulling you close in the dim moonlight
We are lost in our silence tonight

[Verse 1]
The porch swing sways in the midnight breeze
Your touch is a whisper that brings me to my knees
Out on the edge of this sleepy old town
There is nowhere to go when the sun goes down

[Pre-Chorus]
Every breath is a spark in the dark
Leaving a mark on this wandering heart
I feel the rhythm of your skin on mine
We are crossing over the invisible line

[Chorus]
Lost in our silence, where the words disappear
Everything I need is standing right here
A slow-burning fire, a secret we keep
In the deep of the night while the whole world is asleep

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, drifting away, just you and me
(Just you and me)

[Verse 2]
Dust on the window, the headlights fade
We are living inside this heaven we made
Your eyes are a map to a place I belong
Writing our story like a favorite song

[Bridge]
Time is standing still inside this room
Caught in the scent of your sweet perfume
It is just Saeed and the love we have found

[Spoken]
There is nothing left to say
Just let the quiet pull us away
